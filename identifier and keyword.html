all js variablesare identified by unique names.These unique name called identifier

Identifier naming rules

  • identifier can have 1 and more than one character
  • 1st character be an alphabet,unerscore(_),dollar symbol($)
  • number is not allowed as 1st character
  • keywords and reserved words should not use as identifier
